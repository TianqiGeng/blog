Lesson37：The Olympic Games 奥林匹克运动会¶

  1. immense adj 巨大的
  • pantastic 巨大的
  • big lage huge vast
  • enormous tremendous colossal
  • gigantic titanic
  1. by the end of this year
    by+时间①与完成时联用@截止到…时间②将来
  2. workers will have completed the now roads
  • 将来完成时，仅适用第一人称
  • 形式：will shallt have done
  • 用法：站在将来住前看
  • By the end of next year I shall have been here on the job
  1. Everybody will be watching as the new building go up 时间状语，一般现在表将来
  2. ..are looking forward to the Olympic Games.
  3. will be done 一般将来时的被动
  • will be doing 将来进行时
  • will have done 将来完成时……时间之前(L5 L7)
